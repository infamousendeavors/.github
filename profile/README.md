# Infamous Endeavors

Software, games, and other ideas that wouldn't leave me alone.

Infamous Endeavors is Joe Garcia's maker label. iOS apps, CLI tools, MCP servers,
games, and whatever else wouldn't stay quiet until it got built. The medium
changes. The person behind it doesn't.

## What's shipping

| Project | What it is |
|---|---|
| [Lunch Money MCP](https://lunchmoney.infamousendeavors.com) | MCP server for the Lunch Money API. Ask your agent how much you actually spent on coffee. |
| JobSite | Concrete calculator for the field. Built for the guy in boots, not the guy in a spreadsheet. |
| PDF Split & Merge | Split and merge PDFs entirely on your phone. Nothing leaves your device. |

## How we build

- No ad networks, no cross-app tracking, no fingerprinting, no selling your data.
- Local-first where it makes sense. Plain-English privacy and terms.
- Open source where it helps. MIT by default.
